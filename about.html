What Is Atixa?

Atixa is a free, browser-based quiz platform designed to help curious people test, expand, and celebrate their knowledge. Whether you're a student brushing up before an exam, a trivia enthusiast looking for your next challenge, or simply someone who enjoys learning — Atixa has a quiz for you.

We cover six core knowledge domains: General Knowledge, Science, Technology, History, Mathematics, and English Vocabulary — with questions carefully researched and written to be fair, accurate, and genuinely interesting.

💡 Our promise: Atixa will always be free to use, require no account, and never sell your personal data. We are supported by non-intrusive advertising and voluntary newsletter subscriptions.

Mission & Vision

🎯
Our Mission
To make high-quality, engaging knowledge tests available to anyone with an internet connection — completely free, with no barriers to entry. We exist to spark curiosity and reward the act of learning.
🔭
Our Vision
To become the world's most-loved free quiz platform — a place where millions of learners visit daily to challenge themselves, track their growth, and discover the joy of knowing things.
📖
Our Values
Accuracy over quantity. Simplicity over clutter. Respect for the user over aggressive monetisation. Every decision we make is filtered through these three principles.
🤝
Our Commitment
We commit to keeping the platform ad-free of intrusive formats, GDPR-compliant, and continuously improving question quality based on community feedback.

Why Trust Atixa?

Trust is not given — it's earned through consistent, transparent behaviour. Here is why thousands of users return to Atixa every day:

  • No hidden fees or paywalls. Every question, category, and feature is completely free.
  • No account required. Start a quiz within seconds. We don't collect your name, email, or any personal identifier just to let you learn.
  • Research-backed questions. All questions are reviewed against reliable, publicly available sources before publication.
  • Transparent data practices. We publish a plain-English Privacy Policy and Cookie Policy. We do not sell data to third parties.
  • Regular updates. Our question bank is refreshed regularly to fix errors, add new categories, and improve difficulty calibration.

Our Technology

Atixa is intentionally built with zero dependencies — no heavyweight frameworks, no tracking SDKs loaded on page start, no third-party cookies by default. The entire platform runs on clean HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript, making it fast on any device and any connection speed.

This approach also means our codebase is auditable, lightweight, and will keep working reliably for years without maintenance headaches.
