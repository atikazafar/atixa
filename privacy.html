Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

📅 Last updated: January 1, 2026 🌍 Applies globally ✅ GDPR compliant

Summary: We do not sell your personal data. We do not require you to register to use Atixa. Any data collected (such as anonymous usage statistics) is used solely to improve the platform and serve relevant advertisements.

1. Who We Are

Atixa ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at https://atixa.app (the "Site"). We are the data controller for the purposes of applicable data protection legislation, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@atixa.app

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide Directly

When you use our contact form or subscribe to our newsletter, we collect:

  • Your email address (newsletter subscription)
  • Your name and message content (contact form submissions)

This data is provided voluntarily. You are not required to provide it to use the quiz platform.

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

When you visit our Site, we and our third-party service providers (including Google Analytics and Google AdSense) may automatically collect:

  • IP address (anonymised where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search query
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Quiz interaction data (category selected, score ranges — not linked to individuals)

2.3 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies. Please read our Cookie Policy for full details. In summary:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g., theme preference).
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data to help us improve the Site.
  • Advertising cookies: Google AdSense places cookies to show relevant advertisements. These may use your browsing history across other sites.
Advertisement

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate and improve the Atixa platform
  • To respond to your contact form messages
  • To send newsletters, if you have subscribed (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To serve relevant, personalised advertisements via Google AdSense
  • To analyse usage trends and improve user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and UK, our legal bases for processing personal data are:

  • Consent: For advertising cookies, analytics cookies, and newsletter emails. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests: For basic security monitoring, fraud prevention, and anonymous analytics.
  • Contract: To respond to your contact form enquiries.
  • Legal obligation: Where required by law.

5. Advertising — Google AdSense

We participate in the Google AdSense programme. Google and its partners use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this Site and other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our Site and/or other sites on the internet. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

6. Analytics — Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our Site. Google Analytics collects anonymised data. We have enabled IP anonymisation on our Google Analytics implementation. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

7. Third-Party Services

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites or embed third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before submitting any personal data.

Current third-party services used:

  • Google Analytics (analytics)
  • Google AdSense (advertising)
  • Google Fonts (typography — fonts are loaded from Google servers)

8. Data Retention

  • Newsletter emails: Retained until you unsubscribe.
  • Contact form submissions: Retained for up to 24 months, then deleted.
  • Analytics data: Retained for 26 months in Google Analytics (standard setting).
  • Cookies: As described in our Cookie Policy.

9. Your Rights (GDPR & Similar)

Where applicable, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restrict processing — request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a portable format.
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw consent at any time for consent-based processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@atixa.app. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

Atixa is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA). If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

11. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the Site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, complaints, or requests, please contact our Data Protection contact at:
